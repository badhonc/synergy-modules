"POS Software"

Why You Need a Point-of-Sale Software

Managing Orders & Inventory Manually is crucial and inefficient. With an user-friendly, easy-to-use POS Software, your business management process will be smoother & easier than ever.

Feature List

Features of Our POS Module

Multiple Vendor Management

From here you can add your local or international Vendors information. These Vendors would be associated with products that are purchased.

Learn More
Multiple Warehouse Management

From here you can add & edit your Warehouse information. Multiple Warehouses can be inserted. Reports can be generated on Warehouse basis.

Learn More
Multiple Branch Management

From here you can add your Business Branch information. Reports can be generated on Branch basis.

Learn More
Products Management

From here you can add your Products information. Multiple Categories, Subcategories, & other relevant information can be inserted for a single product.

Learn More
Discount & Coupon

You can add product based discount & coupon. Discount would be calculated based on coupon value or inserted discount amount.

Learn More
VAT & TAX Management

VAT & TAX can be easily integrated with this module. Final report would be calculated considering the VAT & TAX amount inserted

Learn More
Purchase & Sale Bill

Bill entry is mandatory for order and stock management. You can easily enter purchase & sale bills. Sale bill entry has two types of customer info. One is old customers, and the other is new walk-in customer. Purchase/Sale report would be generated based on these bills.

Learn More
Customer Management

Customer Management is very easy with our system. Regular Customers can be selected while selling or issuing product, and New customer can be registered at the same time. Customer Database is created through this process from which HR Management starts to roll.

Learn More
Bar Code Management

Bar Code can be generated when making a sale. That bar code will contain product information. The system is fully automated.

Learn More
Report Generation

Various kinds of reports can be generated, i.e. Opening Stock Report, Purchase Report, Sale Report, Stock Report, Availability Report.
